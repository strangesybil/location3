location, location, location

is a site-specific radio play about an interreligious couple living in flatbush. miriam believes in justice. richie“believes” in God. they’re engaged,exhausted, and planning a wedding neither of them fully understands. location, location, location follows a lapsed muslim and a wavering catholic as they navigate the holy mess of guilt, grief, and grace.
